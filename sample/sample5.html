Trivia

The man on the seat in the card artwork is former USA President Abraham Lincoln, as represented in the statue in the Lincoln Memorial building in Washington, D.C. The stars and stripes background is a part of the USA flag. The five "ghost" images of Lincoln appear in the colors red, white, green, black, and blue - which are the five colors of magic in the Wizards of the Coast game Magic: The Gathering.
